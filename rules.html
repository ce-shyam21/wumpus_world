👻Wumpus World👻



🎯 Introduction

Welcome to the 👻Wumpus World👻, where you navigate through a mysterious cave system filled with dangers ☢️.

🎯 Objective

Your goal is to navigate 🧭 the cave, avoid hazards 🚨, and find the hidden treasure 🪙🎁.

🎯 Gameplay

  • You control a character exploring a grid-based cave.🏁
  • Each room may contain hazards like pits 🕳️ or the fearsome Wumpus 👻.
  • Use clues and logic 💭to determine safe paths 🛟 and locate the treasure 🪙🎁.
  • Beware: making the wrong move could lead to danger or defeat!☢️❌
  • Initally all The grid are marked red 🔴
  • Node that are visited by the player will be convarted in to green 🟢 for help to go back

🎯 Rules

  • The rooms adjacent to the Wumpus👻 room are smelly, so that it would have some stench ♨️.
  • The room adjacent to PITs 🕳️ has a breeze ❄️, so if the agent reaches near to PIT 🕳️, then he will perceive the breeze.
  • There will be glitter✨ in the room if and only if the room has gold🪙🥇.(Win the Game🏆)
  • Beware: The Agent🧔 can be killed by the Wumpus👻 if the agent is facing to it, and Wumpus will emit a horrible scream which can be heard anywhere in the cave.

🎯 Controls

Use given buttons ⬆️⬇️⬅️➡️ click on adjacent rooms to move your character.
