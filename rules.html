Introduction

Welcome to the Wumpus World, where you navigate through a mysterious cave system filled with dangers.

Objective

Your goal is to navigate the cave, avoid hazards, and find the hidden treasure.

Gameplay

  • You control a character exploring a grid-based cave.
  • Each room may contain hazards like pits or the fearsome Wumpus.
  • Use clues and logic to determine safe paths and locate the treasure.
  • Beware: making the wrong move could lead to danger or defeat!

Rules

  • The rooms adjacent to the Wumpus room are smelly, so that it would have some stench.
  • The room adjacent to PITs has a breeze, so if the agent reaches near to PIT, then he will perceive the breeze.
  • There will be glitter in the room if and only if the room has gold.(Win the Game)
  • Beware: The Wumpus can be killed by the agent if the agent is facing to it, and Wumpus will emit a horrible scream which can be heard anywhere in the cave.

Controls

Use arrow keys or click/tap on adjacent rooms to move your character.
